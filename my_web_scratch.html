How I Started

I first got into triathlons in high school when my dad restored his bike and we started riding together. At the time I was already doing a lot of running, so we decided to try a triathlon together. That experience sparked my interest in the sport and I have continued training ever since.

Before triathlon I participated in several sports:

  1. Sports I competed in
    • Cross Country
    • Swimming
    • Basketball
    • Volleyball

My Weekly Training

I train about 10 hours per week and try to balance swimming, biking, and running.

  1. Running
    • Monday – Intervals or hills
    • Friday – Long run
  2. Swimming
    • Tuesday – Swim workout
    • Thursday – Swim workout
  3. Cycling
    • Wednesday – Bike ride
    • Saturday – Long bike ride

The hardest part of training is spacing out my hard workouts because I tend to push myself too often.

Favorite Race

My favorite race so far was a Half Ironman distance race at Echo Reservoir in Utah. The long bike ride was incredibly scenic and the run course followed a flat dirt path, which made the race both beautiful and enjoyable.

My next big goal is competing in a Half Ironman in Boise, Idaho. Triathlon has taught me that having a clear plan makes a huge difference in achieving goals.
